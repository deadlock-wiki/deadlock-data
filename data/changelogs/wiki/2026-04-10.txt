[ General ]

* Parrying is now allowed while ground dashing in order to bring it in-line with air dashing

* {{AbilityIcon|Parry}} anti mash protection previously would last for 0.25s after the [[Parry|parry]] success (so you couldn't [[Parry|parry]] again for that duration). It is now for 0.25s only after the initial [[Parry|parry]] input is provided, irrespective of when the [[Parry|parry]] success happens (this means there is less delay before you can do a back to back [[Parry|parry]] when [[Parry|parry]] success is done later in the duration)

* Medic [[Trooper]] missing health percent heal reduced from 16% to 14%
* Medic [[Trooper]] fixed regen growth per minute reduced from 4 to 3

* Subsequent CC reduction increased from 10%/30% to 15/35% (window from 8s to 9s)
* Improved various abilities behavior when casting while sliding
