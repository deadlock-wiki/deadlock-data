[ General ]

* [[Trooper]] bounty [[Mechanics|mechanics]] have been reworked. The first half of the [[Souls|souls]] still fly out as deniable [[Orb|orb]], however rather than the other half being instantly given to nearby players it will fall to the ground as an [[Orb|orb]] that you need to get near to pick up. It has a 18m capture radius where it'll travel to you once you get within. Only one player needs to claim it, share rules remain the same as before.
* They last for 18s on the ground. Duration increases by 2s per minute up to 40s after 10 minutes.
* Enemy orbs are visible as faint colorless [[Soul|soul]] orbs to your team, but cannot be claimed.
* Amount of [[Souls|souls]] in the flying [[Orb|orb]] that can be secured/denied reduced from 50% to 40%
* Very small tweaks to the secure/deny lag calculation to improve it
* Increased [[Trooper|trooper]] range to be considered in range for [[Souls|souls]] to drop from 35m to 45m
* Medic [[Troopers|troopers]] no longer have a passive [[Healing|healing]] pulse [[Ability|ability]]
* Medic [[Troopers|troopers]] now drop a medic pack when you kill them. Has same pickup range/duration [[Mechanics|mechanics]] as the [[Soul|soul]] orbs on the ground. Heals everyone in a 30m radius for 10% of their missing [[Health|health]] as well as 75 plus 2 per minute game time.
