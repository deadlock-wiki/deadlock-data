[ General ]

* [[Soul]] Orbs no longer have special duplication rules before 8 minutes. [[Soul]] splitting rules are now the same before 8 minutes as afterwards. Adjusted [[Trooper|trooper]] economy to compensate. There is also no longer a bounty bump that occurs at 8 minutes.

* Wave interval increased from 25s to 30s (bounty values adjusted by the same ratio)
* [[Guardians]] attack range reduced from 38.1m to 35m
* [[Guardians]] [[Melee|melee]] attempt range reduced from 6.1m to 5m
* [[Guardians]] [[Melee Damage|melee damage]] range reduced from 8m to 7m
* Players can no longer [[Parry]] [[Troopers|troopers]]
* Medic [[Trooper]]: Heal no longer has [[Charges|charges]] (was 3)
* Medic [[Trooper]]: [[Cooldown]] reduced from 35s to 6s (this was the previous setup before)
* Medic [[Trooper]]: Fixed [[Healing|healing]] sometimes not functioning on bounce when there are walls nearby
* [[Troopers]] will no longer aggro to you when you shoot them while they are attacking a [[Guardian]]

* [[Urn]] now reveals you immediately
* First [[Urn|urn]] is now considered for comeback [[Mechanics|mechanics]] (15% NW delta)
* [[Urn]] now has a visual indication on the model to indicate if its a side favored pickup
* [[Urn]] will no longer wait indefinitely for someone nearby to pick him up. He'll ignore loiterers after 20 seconds in aggregate and start running back home
* Fixed [[Debuff]] Resistance affecting [[Urn]] pickup time

* Heavy [[Melee]] Cancel momentum is significantly reduced



[ [[Trooper]] AI Fixes ]

* [[Melee]] [[Troopers|troopers]] will no longer see and follow players around corners and through veils
* Smoothed out [[Trooper|trooper]] navigation in some spots of the [[Map|map]] where they need to drop down
* [[Troopers]] that are attacking [[Shrines|shrines]] are no longer distracted from their task by distant enemy [[Troopers|troopers]]
* [[Troopers]] now know to cross the base and attack the other [[Shrine|shrine]] once their near one is defeated
* Fixed a bug where [[Troopers|troopers]] would rapidly change targets when multiple enemies are in range
* Fixed a bug where [[Troopers|troopers]] would fidget with their facing direction when [[Walking|walking]] without a target
* Fixed a bug where [[Troopers|troopers]] would fear really steep stairs
* Fixed a bug where [[Troopers|troopers]] would flash their targeting flare at the targeted player every time they came into camera or line of sight
