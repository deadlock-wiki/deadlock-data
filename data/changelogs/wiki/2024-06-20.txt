[ General Changes ]

* Added EU servers and matchmaking region. EU testing times are Mon-Thur 5PM to 3AM and Fri-Sun 3PM to 3AM. Times listed are Central European Summer Time (GMT+2).

* Removed the invite requirement of Steam friends having been on your list for a number of days (affected normal user cases often where friends weren't on the Steam list, and we have other metrics and tools on the backend now that we can use to filter for other cases)
* Added search functionality to the shop. You can now search by item name or by individual stat such as 'Ammo', 'Lifesteal', or 'Spirit Power'.
* The game will now let you know when it is safe to abandon (usually if one person has been disconnected for a while)

* Your personal builds are now always sorted to the top of the in-game Builds dropdown
* Various network related improvements when there is packet loss
* Preferred heroes in your roster are now remember on the client across sessions
* Fixed chat wheel not working during the post-game sequence

* Updated the entrance to the underground tunnels from the outer lanes to look more like a subway entrance
* Re-enabled ultra wide UI mode
* Added street names and symbols to the Zipline UI
* Updated {{AbilityIcon|Sleep Dagger}} and Silence Glyph tooltips to clarify that they do not cancel channeling abilities
* Updated reload animations for {{HeroIcon|Warden}}, {{HeroIcon|Dynamo}}, and {{HeroIcon|Ivy}}
* Improved visibility of Inferno's {{AbilityIcon|Concussive Combustion}}
* Fixed the unit target UI not showing up for instant-cast abilities (MoKrill ult, {{HeroIcon|Lady Geist}} ult, {{HeroIcon|Bebop}} bomb)
* Fixed the unit target UI not showing the activation button hint
* Added a Cosmic Veil to the hero demo map
* Added {{ItemIcon|Ethereal Shift}} sounds
* Added wind sound when on the Zipline



[ Misc Gameplay ]

* Rejuv buff is now only granted to the players that are alive. Players that are dead will have their respawn times cut in half from their current values.

* Footsteps are now quieter when obstructed by level geometry
* {{AbilityIcon|Dash}}-Down is now double tap CTRL instead of CTRL+SHIFT
* Vault bounty increased from 300 to 330
* Fixed various bugs with {{ItemIcon|Headshot Booster}} and {{ItemIcon|Sharpshooter}}

* Added another entrance/exit to the underground tunnels from the warehouse interiors
* Enclosed the exterior Medium Neutral Camp with fencing in front of the Theater to match the Amber side Cab Stand
* Added two more veils on each side:
1) Between the Drug Store and the Market on Amber, between the Hotel and Pharmacy on Sapphire
2) Between the exterior Strong Neutral Camps and the outer lane near the T2
