[ General Changes ]

* Playtesting times are now starting 1 hour earlier. Weekdays are 5PM PDT and weekends are 3PM PDT.

* Added in-game custom [[Item|item]] builds support. Create your own builds or browse builds from the community in the [[Heroes|heroes]] page.
* Added post game player reporting option

* Ally player glow-through-world silhouette now communicates their current [[Health|health]]
* Added ambient [[Music|music]] track
* Added unique {{HeroIcon|Haze}} footstep sound effects
* Fixed various issues with mic audio input and lag it introduced
* Small adjustments to how the crosshair UI area looks
* Mic input device is now selected from in-game settings rather than the steam client
* Improved various networking and hit registration related issues (this was released a couple days ago)
* Updated [[Trooper]] and [[Neutral]] shooting sound effects
* Various improvements to [[Damage|damage]] audio clarity for victims and attackers
* Sped up select [[Amber Hand]] VO that related to game state ([[Shrines|shrines]] being under attack, protecting the [[Urn|urn]] runner etc)
* Adding game audio muted indicator in top left if the game is muted
* Camera can now be moved while the game is paused in replays
* Fixed the [[Rejuvenator]] falling during pause
* Fixed the Idol falling during pause
* Fixed the bottom most [[Shop|shop]] [[Items|items]] having part of their icon be unclickable
* Fixed a few [[Unit|unit]] target [[Abilities|abilities]] that could sometimes select a target behind a nearby wall (e.g. {{HeroIcon|Bebop}} {{AbilityIcon|Sticky Bomb}})
* Added some additional visual tells for the receiving end of {{HeroIcon|Lash}}’s {{AbilityIcon|Death Slam}}
* Updated Bullet Shredder [[Debuff|debuff]] effects
* [[Mid-Boss]] is now invulnerable if there are no enemies it can actually attack
* Fixed your [[Active Items|active items]] not hiding in the UI when you are dead and spectating a team mate
* Added support for binding mouse1 and mouse2 to any key (this was released a couple days ago)
* Fixed various [[Movement|movement]] stuck bugs with {{HeroIcon|Ivy}}
* Added alt info to recent [[Damage|damage]] view to make it clearer that this can be brought up again after it disappears
* Improved {{HeroIcon|Vindicta}}’s {{AbilityIcon|Stake}} effects to make it a little clearer when you are affected
* Fixed upgrading {{HeroIcon|McGinnis}}’s {{AbilityIcon|Medicinal Specter}} to T3 while [[Active|active]] causing it to heal everyone to full instantly
* Fixed a bug that could cause all of {{HeroIcon|Paradox}} {{AbilityIcon|Pulse Grenade}}’s pulses to go off at once
* Fixed {{HeroIcon|Kelvin}}’s Ice Beam sometimes not hitting [[Neutrals]]
* Fixed being able to see some things through the Veils in the [[Map|map]] that you shouldn’t be able to see
* Removed [[Stack|stack]] of cardboard boxes inside connection hallway near bases to prevent cover from [[Neutrals]]
* Fixed getting stuck under stair at inner [[Lanes|lanes]] near [[Guardian]]
* Fixed {{HeroIcon|Abrams}} getting stuck on department store facade when using his ult
* Sapphire side materials tint color changed to white
* Sapphire side street materials changed to new asphalt blend
* Added new slide and reload animation for {{HeroIcon|Wraith}}
* Out of combat run animation variants now only play during [[Sprint|sprint]] state
* Fixed missing VO for {{HeroIcon|Yamato}} [[Ability|ability]] use/upgrades
* Allies now compliment teammates when they see impressive use of channeled [[Abilities|abilities]] (for instance, Gigawatt killing three people with {{AbilityIcon|Storm Cloud}})
* Adjusted timing of select {{HeroIcon|Abrams}} impact lines to time with either the rise of his leap or the descent accordingly

* Improved visibility of {{HeroIcon|Seven}}’s bullet and travel path
* Improved visibility of {{HeroIcon|Paradox}}’s bullet and travel path
* Improved visibility of {{HeroIcon|Wraith}}’s bullet and travel path
* Improved visibility of {{HeroIcon|Haze}}’s bullet and travel path
* Improved visibility of {{HeroIcon|Lady Geist}}’s bullet and travel path
* Added custom tracer and muzzle flash for {{HeroIcon|Seven}}’s gun
* Added custom tracer and muzzle flash for {{HeroIcon|Paradox}}’s gun
* Added custom tracer and muzzle flash for {{HeroIcon|Wraith}}’s gun



[ Misc Gameplay Changes ]

* Adjusted [[Map|map]] around the middle [[Lanes|lanes]] to have an area with Veils for laning
* [[Hero]] kill bounty ratio between Killer and Assister reduced from 2.75x to 2.55x (means slightly more to the Assister)
* [[Spirit]] scaling reduced by ~15% (the total effective [[Spirit damage]] is reduced by less than this, this is just the [[Spirit]] multiplier scaling portion and has largest impact on late game burst)

* Unclaimed [[Souls|souls]] now convert 10% slower
* Vaults base bounty increased from 220 to 260
* Strong [[Neutrals]] [[Spirit Resist]] increased from 25% to 35%
* Medium [[Neutrals]] [[Spirit Resist]] increased from 25% to 30%
* Starting at 15 minutes, one [[Melee|melee]] [[Trooper]] per wave will spawn with 1.75x [[Health|health]]
* Medic [[Trooper]] [[Health|health]] increased from 240 to 260
* Sidelane Veils near [[Guardians]] are now one-way visibility (used to be blocked visibility in both directions)
* [[Neutrals]] can now lean side to side to fire when obstructed by [[Map|map]] geometry and being attacked

* Zipline Boost [[Ability|ability]] speed increased from 100% to 130%
* Zipline Boost [[Ability|ability]] ramp time to reach max speed reduced from 2s to 1s
* Zipline base speed boost after Base [[Guardians]] are killed increased from 100% to 120%
* Zipline base speed boost's ramp time after Base [[Guardians]] are killed reduced from 5s to 4s
* Being Disarmed no longer prevents you from reloading
