General Changes:
==
* Added a Recommend A Friend button to the dashboard that you can use to send us requests for people to include in our playtesting
* Added a Resources page to the dashboard which contains a browsable [[Item|item]] [[Shop|shop]]
* Added overhead text display when another [[Hero|hero]] uses [[Active Items|active items]]
* The hotkeys F1-F5 to change cameras to allied [[Heroes|heroes]] now maps directly to the order of [[Heroes|heroes]] on the top bar left to right
* Added the [[Patron]] to the spectate-when-dead cycle if the enemy is in your base or everyone on your team is dead
* Added support for [[Flex]] [[Items]] in the [[Hero]] Sandbox

* Increased the range of the mouse sensitivity slider from 0.5->4.0 to 0.05->8.0
* Increased the default framerate cap from 120 to 400
* Improved UI display when endgame objectives are being attacked
* Added [[Music|music]] for when the base is under attack
* Changed the local player icon on the the [[Minimap|minimap]] to always be on top of enemy icons
* [[Shop]] [[Music|music]] will now only play for shops players can access
* Postgame graphs now default to team [[Stats|stats]] rather than individual
* Added borders around [[Hero|hero]] icons on the [[Minimap|minimap]] when watching replays/spectating
* Top bar now shows incoming players before they are fully connected
* Added console command to hide the bar at the bottom in replays ('citadel_hide_replay_hud 1' to hide it, 'citadel_hide_replay_hud 0' to bring it back)

* Various VOIP improvements
* Fixed open mic threshold
* Fixed various consistency issues with the presentation of [[Souls]] terminology, icons, etc
* Fixed {{AbilityIcon|Flying Cloak}} teleporting players into geometry or out of the world
* Fixed {{ItemIcon|Warp Stone}} teleporting you through geometry
* Fixed {{AbilityIcon|Fixation}} decimal point display
* Fixed [[Parry]] animation not playing
* Fixed various issues with the spectate UI display
* Removed the slow climbing vertical recoil on {{HeroIcon|Yamato}}'s alt fire
* Fixed AP count not showing if you are dead, spectating a team mate or when holding alt/tab to upgrade your [[Abilities|abilities]]
